Why IILM

Apart from imparting quality education and producing thousands of scholars, professionals, and entrepreneurs; IILM has got many feathers into its cap. If you are apprehensive and looking for the right institute to help you grow comprehensively then IILM has many valid points to prove its worth.


Here is a glimpse -


Ecologically Balanced Green Campus

IILM is relentlessly striving to be an ecologically balanced green campus through supporting various environmental sustainability initiatives. Rainwater harvesting, Wastewater treatment, Clean energy through a fully functional 149.5 KW roof-top solar power plant, Bio Composting by students utilizing kitchen & forest waste and Soilless cultivation, are some of the salient features being supported at the campus. We quite proudly nurture diverse flora and fauna including migratory birds that get attracted by our campus’s rich foliage & recycled water resources.

Technology Hot-Spot, Centrally Located

Our latest technology enabled Hi-Tech campus is centrally located in the heart of knowledge hub-Greater Noida. It has 18 Acre Lush Green, Eco-Friendly, Wi-Fi enabled Campus with mini Golf Course, Basketball court, Volley Ball court, and a Gymnasium inside to facilitate you're both physical and intellectual needs.

IILM-CET Innovation Centre

Our ultimate focus is to 'promote culture for innovation', give due recognition to students who have come out with creative ideas and support them towards becoming successful entrepreneurs through our Entrepreneurship Incubation Model.

At IILM, we-
• Encourage students to take part in innovation, idea generation & product development
• Facilitate innovative products that may be useful for society
• Provide support for bringing student’s innovative idea into a product
• Exhibit product output of student innovation projects

‘Stars’ on our shoulders-
Student Innovations at IILM Engineering have proved their mettle at many nodes-
•  NASA Rover Challenge, USA - Winning Team
•  Development of an Eco-friendly Hybrid car

Moreover, IILM is also associated with NEN, TIE and partnering institution of NIESBUD to formally recognize our entrepreneurial driven culture.

Our Faculties - Our Resource

IILM has staffed more than 80 full-time faculties from the best academic institutions like IIT, BITS, JNU, IIM, DU who carry with them a rich industrial and research experience. Our teaching staff dedicatedly work as per the students need, easily connect with the students and are equally assertive in imparting value-based education.

State of the Art Laboratories

In order to aid your theoretical learning and facilitate innovative research, we are in a steady process of upgrading our laboratories with world-class infrastructure and excellent R&D facilities. Our Lab facilities are NBA accredited, thereby recognized as excellent infrastructure maintained by dedicated, skilled and experienced supporting staff.

A Network of 9000+ Well placed Alumni

IILM has a diverse conglomeration of well-placed Alumni working with top-notch firms in India and Abroad. We organize regular alma connect sessions for promoting interaction of current batches with the alumni and serve a platform to connect them with each other.

Outstanding Placements & Best Internships

With over 200+ leading companies across the Nation visit IILM, Greater Noida campus every year. A wide array of Topmost firms like Wipro, HCL, Accenture, Mankind Pharma, Syntel, Nutrilife, Cordlife, Biocell and many others have happily staffed IILM undergraduates. Thousands of IILM pass outs are also excellently serving many other apex industries worldwide.

On-Campus Comfortable Residences

To support your peaceful and safe stay we have kept on-campus residential facility for students and faculty. For 500+ students. Air-conditioned, Wi-Fi enabled hostel with power back up your best comfort is assured with us.

Safe and Secure Campus

Entire IILM campus is under CCTV surveillance. We have a robust security team, ready round the clock to prevent any undesirable moments. IILM is the safest campus in Greater Noida region. Our Hostel Security is specifically managed by veteran ex-commandos of the Indian Army. Hence, our standards are naturally well above averages.

Moreover, there are a lot many life experiences, worth celebrating at IILM CET!
Like to visit us! Just call or stop by, we’d be proud to show you around!!
