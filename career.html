Career Guidance and Placement Cell

Career Guidance and Placement Cell (CGPC) at IILM Academy of Higher Learning, College of Engineering & Technology, Greater Noida (IILM CET) provides gainful insight to students about various career options available. It hones & enhance their skill and competencies to help them choose the right career.

CGPC is constantly in touch with the Corporates/industry to help expose our students to the best of industry practices & knowledge. This further enhances our student’s worth and make them industry ready.

At IILM CET, we have a focused approach towards each student and help them “surpassing corporate expectation”.

The CGPC untiring approach is to dwell and maintain a very strong industry-academia linkage and constantly strive for the overall development & grooming of the students.

Throughout the year, there is a tremendous focus on conducting conclaves, conferences, seminars, guest lectures, workshops, symposia on contemporary topics to further enrich student’s knowledge & skills.

CGPC Activities :

“Everyone has talent.”
 
“We just help sharpen/hone it to realize one’s full worth & true potential.”
 

At IILM CET, we are governed by the above dictum.

We leave no stone unturned to groom and mold our student to prepare for the deserving professionals of tomorrow. Career Guidance & Placement Cell keeps a tab on the existing & futuristic employment opportunities for students.

CGPC puts in extra effort to develop student’s skills. Simultaneously, we work on leadership, teamwork, problem solving, entrepreneurship, integrity, business communication and effective use of modern tools & techniques by them. We also provide the students with placement oriented training to become invaluable contributors in the organizational and the institutional spaces they will eventually inhabit. Making our students industry ready is our sole motto.

Our focus in CGPC is to provide the best available career prospects to the students. We always encourage our students to work on live projects, to help them relate classroom teachings with the industrial practices and also help the Corporate/Industry making it a win-win scenario.

Through our vast network and corporate relations, we in IILM CET have been successfully sourcing relevant job openings and aligning with student’s profile. We firmly believe in holistic development of our students to not only excel in the desired chosen field but also become a good human being and responsible citizen.
