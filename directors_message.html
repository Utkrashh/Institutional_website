Dear Students and Parents,


I am extremely happy to introduce you to IILM College of Engineering and Technology, Greater Noida. IILM College of Engineering and Technology, Greater Noida,  has started its journey in 2002, with the  idea to equip young minds with technical and management skills, along with ethical values, so that they can make a meaningful contribution towards the development of country. At IILM, we believe in the education system which incorporates- professionalism, dedication and honesty. We have achieved the unmatched objective of integrating quality education and innovative thinking with a conscious mind towards social responsibility and professional ethics. I am confident that the consistent efforts of our students, management, faculty and staff members, IILM Engineering College will attain new height of excellence in due course of time.

IILM has its student’s base from all across the country and they are doing really well. Network of Alumni is very strong at IILM as More than 9000 Alumni are working all across the globe. Faculty members working here are well qualified and highly committed towards their responsibilities.

At IILM Engineering College, our focus is on the 360 Degree development of our students and various activities are undertaken apart from curriculum, to meet this objective. Regular mentoring sessions with faculty members and distinguished Alumni are arranged for every student. IILM provide the opportunity to work on Industry linked student projects. On campus guidance/counselling is provided for GATE/GRE/CAT/SSB etc tests. IILM is a student centric institution and our focus is on Student centric T-shape approach where each student is trained for   knowledge and skills in particular area as well as to collaborate with other disciplines and would be able to use and apply knowledge and skills acquired. Every pre final and final year students is mapped with Alumni placed in   reputed organisation or leading his/her venture. Mentoring sessions for students and faculties by Professors from IITs/JNU/NIT and from industry personals are organised on regular basis. IILM is running a program which connects corporate to campus where students are prepared       for Resume writing, technical interviews, group discussions, aptitude and   leadership skills.  Students are encouraged to work on innovative and socially relevant projects. Apart from academics, student clubs like photography, music, dance, art & craft etc are very active at IILM, which help them learn how to work in a team and also help them to learn leadership skills gradually. Students are encouraged to participate in sports activities. Inter-College Cultural, Technical and sports are organised annually.   To instil the quality of a responsible human, a social service cell is managed and run by the team of students with the help of faculty mentors

In short, every educational institution wants to be at No. 1 position but our commitment is towards student’s satisfaction and if our students are happy, we are already No. 1. With these words I thank everyone reading my message and I welcome all to students and parents’ community, to visit IILM College of Engineering & Technology, Greater Noida.


Dr. Jyotsna Singh
Director
IILM College Of Engineering & Technology
